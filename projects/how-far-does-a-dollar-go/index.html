How Far Does a Dollar Go?

Interactive energy travel cost explorer

A dollar today doesn't get you quite as far… but how far is that exactly? And can it be stretched? This tool turns dollars into miles to see how far you can travel. Electric vehicle (EV) are 4x more efficient than gas cars, and gas prices are extremely volatile. Explore how many miles a single dollar can take you!

How far can you get on $1?

Select a state to load local energy prices and weather data, then adjust to compare how many miles you can travel on a single dollar.

Sources

Data is sourced from the U.S. Energy Information Administration, AAA, NOAA and this ScienceDirect article to model EV fuel efficiency given weather. I use the base vehicle efficiencies of 4 miles/kWh and a gas car MPG of 24 to reflect the 2026 U.S. passenger car averages. The idea for this visual was heavily inspired by the excellent work of the Energy Action Network, showcased in their annual report.
